## Day 00 — Hardware Assessment

Today I opened my old ASUS Pro PC for the 
first time in 3 years. The PC was gifted by 
my father and has been sitting unused since 
I got my laptop and Samsung tab. I cleaned 
the dust, removed the old Toshiba 1TB HDD 
and the PCIe WiFi card. The motherboard is 
an ASUS D642MF with an i5 9th Gen CPU and 
16GB Corsair Vengeance DDR4 RAM. The PC is 
in good condition and ready for its new life 
as a homelab server. The Crucial BX500 1TB 
SSD has arrived and is waiting to be installed 
once Airtel Xstream broadband is set up.
